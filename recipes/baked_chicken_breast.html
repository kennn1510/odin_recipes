Baked Chicken Breast

Description

Baked chicken breast is a really easy protein packer. Let's figure out how to make this crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 400 degrees F.
  2. Rub olive oil or butter on the chicken breasts. Sprinkle seasoning and salt.
  3. Bake in oven for approximately 25 to 45 minutes depending on serving size and thickness. Flip halfway through and finish by reading internal temperature is at least 165 degrees F.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
