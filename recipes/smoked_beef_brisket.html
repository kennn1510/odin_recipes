Texas Style Smoked Beef Brisket

Description

Texas Style Smoked Beef Brisket is a simple, mouth watering recipe. Let's hop in and get to enjoying some of the best briskets you'll ever have in your life.

Ingredients

  • Beef brisket meat
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Store brisket in refrigerator until ready for trimming. Remove any silver skin or excess fat.
  2. Mix salt, black pepper, and garlic. Apply on brisket evenly.
  3. Preheat smoker to 225 degrees F. Put brisket in and close lid. Check internal thermometer reads 165 degrees F (Approximately 8 hours).
  4. Wrap brisket in butcher paper or foil. Return wrapped brisket to smoker.
  5. Maintain smoker at 225 degrees F. Wait till internal temperature of brisket is at 202 degrees F.
  6. Remove brisket from smoker and allow to rest for 1 hour. Slice and enjoy!
