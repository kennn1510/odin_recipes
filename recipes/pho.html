Pho

Description

Pho is a popular and delicious Vietnamese rice noodle soup.

Ingredients

  • Beef soup bones
  • Rice noodles
  • Beef
  • Fish sauce
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Star anise
  • Salt
  • Garnishes (anything you would like including cilantro, green onion, Thai basil, lime, hoisin sauce, sriracha, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Roast beef bones in oven till brown for about 1 hour.
  3. Cut onion in half and roast in oven until blackened and soft for about 45 minutes.
  4. Put beef bones and onion into large stockpot. Add ginger, salt, star anise, fish sauce, and water. Bring to a boil and then reduce head to a low simmer for about 8 hours. Strain broth.
  5. Let stockpot settle.
  6. Soak rice noodles in room temperature water for 1 hour and drain.
  7. Cook noodles in boiling water for 1 minute and drain.
  8. Serve with desired garnishes. Watch out, it's hot! Enjoy!
