Pho

Description

Pho is a popular and delicious Vietnamese rice noodle soup.

Ingredients

  • Beef soup bones
  • Rice noodles
  • Beef
  • Fish sauce
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Star anise
  • Salt
  • Garnishes (anything you would like including cilantro, green onion, Thai basil, lime, hoisin sauce, sriracha, etc.)
